About

EKSPERT-M

Our company, EKSPERT-M Co.Ltd. is officially registered at the Ministry of Finance of Azerbaijan as an independent Loss Adjusters, Insurance Surveyors and Cargo Inspection company on April, 2005 .EKSPERT-M Co. Ltd. is a private company providing wide spectre of services on evaluation of cause and extent of the damage occured during transit and storage of goods due to natural disasters, accidents and unlawful acts of third parties, as well as discharging /de-stuffing inspections of miscellaneous cargo ( food and fruits, steel , machines , equipments etc.) from vessels, trucks and containers . We are able to conduct condition and damage surveys of merchant vessels for H&M and P&I insurers at Baku (Azerbaijan) and Turkmenbashi (Turkmenistan).
